Celestial Visitors:

A Visual Exploration of UFO Sightings

Introduction

Unraveling the mysteries of UFO sightings, this visualization presents a comprehensive exploration of reported encounters across the globe. Delving into a dataset spanning various dimensions, including date, location, shape, and encounter duration, this visualization sheds light on intriguing patterns and trends. From geographic hotspots to temporal variations, users can navigate through an array of visualizations using the intuitive menu at the top of the screen. The visualization encompasses diverse views, including choropleth maps sorted by country, revealing intensity of sightings, and stacked bar charts categorizing sightings by region and UFO shape.

Additionally, it unveils temporal insights through trend lines, both by a 24-hour format and month-wise breakdown, providing a deeper understanding of sighting patterns. With this visualization, we embark on an intriguing voyage through the enigmatic world of UFO sightings, offering a multifaceted lens for enthusiasts, researchers, and curious minds alike.

Map

The map shows UFO sightings in each of the different countries.

As you can see, the United States is far and away the leader with the most UFO sightings, followed by Europe. It can also be observed that more sightings occur in developed or crowded parts of the United States. However, less developed countries generally do not have UFO sightings, such as Africa. From these observations, we can conclude that the number of UFO sightings in a country depends on how developed the country or region is.

Tooltips are enabled for the following visualization. Hover over these points to further display eyewitness comments on the event.

Time-based Trends

From January to December, you can see that January is when the most incidents occur, the other months are basically stable around 600-400, and January has close to 1300 UFO sightings.

Regarding the time of day, a consistent trend emerges. Midday, particularly around 11 o'clock, marks a peak in UFO incidents, reaching nearly 1,000 recorded sightings. This suggests a notable concentration of sightings during this time period.

UFO Shape

When it comes to studying UFO shapes, lighting formations take the lead with a staggering 3,343 recorded sightings, followed closely by triangles at 1,577, and circles at 1,568. The least frequently reported shape is the delta, with only a single record.
